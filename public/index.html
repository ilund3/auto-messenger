CSV Format Requirements:

  • Required column: phone (phone numbers should be in format: +1234567890)
  • Any other columns are optional and can be used as placeholders in your messages
  • Use {column_name} in your messages to insert values from any column
  • Examples: {first_name}, {company}, {city}, {use_ai}, {email}, etc.
  • Column names are case-sensitive - use the exact column name from your CSV
  • Rows without phone numbers are automatically skipped
